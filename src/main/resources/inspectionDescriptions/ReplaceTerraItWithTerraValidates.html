This inspection reports usage of Terra.it helpers, and provides quick fixes to replace them with their Terra.validates> counterparts.

All three Terra.it helpers are supported, and based on the presence of before hooks, alternative quick fixes may be available to merge the particular Terra.it with the before hook before it.

For specific examples, please head to the [plugin documentation](https://github.com/picimako/terra-support/blob/master/docs/terra_helpers.md).
